Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my
top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:
  1. Cha Café and Bistro
  2. Savory Bistro Café
  3. Himawari Restaurant
  4. Subway
  5. D'Elegance Café
Cha Café | Garapan, Saipan

Cha Café serves coffee and tea. They also serve food such as baked goods and desserts. It is a nice place to socialize with friends and maybe get work done.
I like it because it is near Micro Beach and American Memorial Park.

What I love:

  • Their tea and coffee
  • Their desserts
  • Their location
